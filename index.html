The plan

Clear route. One Bend basecamp.

One major move after Hood River, three king ensuite rooms near Tetherow, pool and hot tub time, and a week paced around shared meals instead of overstuffed days.

Route map

Portland to the Gorge, over the mountain, into Bend.

This is a north-up route sketch, not a survey map: Portland and Hood River sit along the Gorge, Mount Hood is the optional mountain spur, Bend is the basecamp, Smith Rock is north of Bend, and Crater Lake is the big southbound day.

North-up schematic: Hood River is east of Portland; Mount Hood is the optional southbound spur; Bend is the basecamp; Smith Rock is north of Bend; Crater Lake is south of Bend.

Itinerary

The week at a glance.

Bend choices

Active path or relaxed path.

Mon, Jun 22

Arrival day

Active

Take the Mount Hood / Timberline Lodge detour, then stretch the legs on a short Bend river walk.

Relaxed

Drive straight to Paulina Ridge 32, unpack slowly, use the hot tub, and do an easy dinner.

Tue, Jun 23

Deschutes day

Active

Deschutes River Trail, mountain biking, paddle session, or a 9-hole Tetherow round.

Relaxed

Old Mill lunch, pool time, patio reading, brewery stop, and a slow dinner in Bend.

Wed, Jun 24

Crater Lake day

Active

Rim viewpoints, short viewpoint walks, photos at Watchman Overlook, and Discovery Point.

Relaxed

Choose fewer stops, linger at the best overlooks, bring snacks, and return early for hot tub time.

Thu, Jun 25

Smith Rock day

Active

Misery Ridge / River Trail loop for dramatic canyon and rock spire views.

Relaxed

River Trail out-and-back, scenic overlooks, coffee in Bend, and an unhurried packing afternoon.
